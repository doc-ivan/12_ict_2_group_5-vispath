Interactive Story Submission Rules & Guidelines

Please read carefully before submitting your story

Before uploading your interactive story, please read and follow these rules carefully. Our platform is designed for Grade 12 students, so all content must be appropriate, respectful, and safe for everyone.

1 Appropriate Content Only

  • Stories must be suitable for students aged 16–18.
  • No R18, explicit, or sexually suggestive content.
  • Action, fight scenes, and intense moments are allowed, but avoid detailed gore, extreme violence, or graphic descriptions.
  • Mild suspense, thriller, or emotional drama is allowed as long as it is not disturbing or inappropriate.

2 Original Work

  • Submit only your own original story.
  • Do not copy stories from books, websites, AI platforms, or other authors.
  • Plagiarized or heavily copied content will not be approved.

3 Privacy and Safety

  • Do not share personal information (full name, address, phone number, school ID, passwords).
  • Do not expose private details of other people without permission.
  • Do not include real classmates, teachers, or individuals in a harmful or disrespectful way.

4 Keep It Meaningful

  • Stories should have a clear theme, lesson, or purpose.
  • Creative genres such as action, adventure, comedy, mystery, school life, drama, and realistic fiction are welcome.
  • Avoid nonsense, spam, or extremely low-effort content.

⚠️ Important Note

  • All stories will be reviewed before approval.
  • If your submission does not follow these rules, it may not be approved or may be removed.
  • Repeated violations may result in account restrictions.
Our goal is to create a safe and creative space for students to express their imagination responsibly.
We look forward to your thoughtful and well-crafted submissions.
